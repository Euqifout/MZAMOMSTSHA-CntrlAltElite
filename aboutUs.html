About Us

Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a well rounded education that encourages curiosity , resilience , and a lifelong love of learning . We believe the potential of every child and aim to foster their growth through innovative teaching methods and community involment .

Our Mission

our mission is so develop students with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others and the courage to act on their beliefs . We stress the total development of each child : spiritual , moral intellectual, social , emotional and physical. with the best possible experience by offering high-quality products and exceptional service.

Our Vision

our vision at Mzamomtsha Primary school is to empower students to acquire , demonstrate , articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: RESPECT, TOLERANCE & INCLUSION , and excellence.
